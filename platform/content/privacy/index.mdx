We collect information that you directly provide to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, or communicate with us. This may include:

- Account credentials

## 2. Automated Information Collection
When you access our platform, we may automatically collect certain device and usage data, including:

## 3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information for various purposes:

- To improve, personalize, and expand our services
- To communicate with you regarding updates and security notices
- To monitor and analyze usage patterns

We use industry-standard security measures to safeguard your personal data. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
